Our Vision and Promise

At Prabhat Institute, we are dedicated to carrying forward the rich educational legacy of Dr. Prabhat Kumar Saha. Our promise is to provide a sound academic atmosphere with the help of well-trained and dedicated teachers who offer personalized guidance to every student.

To ensure academic excellence, we offer:

  • Supplementary tutorial sessions after school hours.
  • Support to help students consolidate their knowledge.

Our core mission is to instill strong moral values, guiding our students to become responsible and compassionate individuals who can contribute positively to society.

Our Mission & Promise

Established on 2020, Prabhat Institute is an English medium CBSE (NCERT curriculum) school, dedicated to carrying forward the educational legacy of Dr. Prabhat Kumar Saha. We promise to impart modern and affordable education in a sound academic atmosphere with the help of well-trained and dedicated teachers.

To ensure academic excellence, we offer supplementary tutorial sessions after school hours, enabling students to consolidate their knowledge and excel academically. Our core mission is to instill strong moral values, guiding our students to become responsible and compassionate members of society.
